duck chess

i'm working on a chess-like browser game.

i'm building the backend with rust and the frontend with next.js. the basic functionality works, but i have a lot more planned for it.

done

  • simple matchmaking
  • text chat between players during a game
  • server generates moves and client can move their pieces
  • nice animations for pieces moving
  • standard chess pieces

roadmap

  • silly chess pieces
    • a piece that causes gravity to shift the whole board
    • a piece that can "steal" the moveset of an adjacent piece
    • a piece that changes the colors of the tiles it lands on (bishops still can't leave their colors)
    • a piece that leaves ice that sliding pieces can slip on
  • deckbuilding
    • you should be able to choose which pieces you start with
  • more sophisicated matchmaking

it isn't at the stage where i would feel comfortable deploying it, but you can watch me build it on my github.
